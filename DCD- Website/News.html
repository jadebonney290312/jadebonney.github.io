Club Showcase 2022

23/3/2022

Our annual SA Showcase will take place on April 10th at the Knocknarea Arena from 10am-1pm. The event will showcase all of our athletes hard work this season through restrictions and closures and show off a small part of what we've learned this year. Our special guests are Team Ireland Coed Elite, the Irish National Cheerleading Team and all funds raised will be donated to this team going to represent the country at Worlds this April. There will be a bake sale, and a raffle on the day with lots of great prizes and all to help our national athletes. Tickets for spectators are available on the day but seats are limited so come early to avoid standing all day!
